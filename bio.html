I first opened PhotoShop back in 2013, on one of the older versions, CS4. It was purely an outlet for me to be creative. I hated painting and drawing because I was no good at it. All through school, I made small graphics and posters - no birthday card was bought throughout this time. Once I graduated school, I took time to work and think about what I wanted to study and make a career out of. Graphic Design was always a hobby but I'd never considered working in that field until 2017.
I did a PLC course in Blackrock Further Institute of Education where I was introduced to Illustrator and web design and everything else that came along with it. When I graduated with all distinctions, I decided that I both loved and was good at this. Through the course in TU Dublin, I also fell in love with photography and sometimes create graphics from the pictures I take.
Ultimately, it's a labour of love.
