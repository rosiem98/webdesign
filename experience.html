overview

My experience may be limited when it comes to the big, wide world. I have worked for a few small businesses creating graphics for their websites or for advertisements.

I have also undertaken a lot of diverse and different assignments in college to prepare me for this big, wide world. You can see examples of my work throughout this portfolio website and I'm always happy to share more.

graphics

Graphic Design has been at the heart of college course and PLC course. It's always been a hobby of mine. I've uploaded some work to RedBubble, a design website that allows you to create a variety of things (think mugs, t shirts, phone cases and more!) with your designs and sell them. I've helped a few friends and family out with websites and website graphics, aswell as advertising posters.

photography

Photography was never something I meant to love. I had always enjoyed taking pictures for the sake of memories, but it wasn't until my first year of college that I realised I loved taking pictures. Eventually, when I got my hands on my own camera, I started taking pictures to use in my own graphics. I've also helped out a small business by taking images of their stock for their website.
